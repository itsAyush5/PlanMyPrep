# 📘 PlanMyPrep

> Smart AI-powered exam preparation planner built with Google Opal

---

## 📌 Overview

**PlanMyPrep** is a lightweight AI app that generates a personalized study plan based on your exam timeline and available study hours.

Instead of manually figuring out what to study each day, the app creates a structured preparation roadmap tailored to your inputs.

---

## 🎯 What It Does

PlanMyPrep takes the following inputs:

- 📅 Exam date  
- 📚 Subject names  
- 📝 Reference notes / syllabus  
- ⏱ Daily study hours  

Using this information, it generates:

- ✅ Day-wise study schedule  
- ✅ Subject distribution based on time available  
- ✅ Balanced workload to avoid burnout  
- ✅ Structured revision planning  

---

## ⚙️ How It Works

1. User enters exam details and study constraints  
2. The app processes inputs using AI (Gemini via Google Opal)  
3. A structured preparation plan is generated  
4. Output is optimized for clarity and practical execution  

---

## 🖥 Usage

1. Open the app  
   👉 https://opal.google/app/1cgKfEqnkYl1vaqUCW1nA_RP37pHLCN2W

## 📷 Example

**Input:**
- Exam Date: 30 days from now  
- Subjects: Math, Physics, Chemistry  
- Study Time: 5 hours/day  

**Output:**
- Daily schedule dividing subjects  
- Weekly revision slots  
- Gradual increase in intensity closer to exam
